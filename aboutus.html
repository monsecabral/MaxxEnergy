Who is Maxx Energy?

Maxx Energy is a leading provider of energy solutions, specializing in power generation, energy management, and consulting services for businesses, industrial sectors, and government agencies. With a strong focus on sustainability, the company prioritizes renewable energy sources and energy efficiency, leveraging advanced technologies like smart grids and data analytics to optimize energy use and reduce environmental impact. Maxx Energy is committed to delivering innovative, reliable, and sustainable energy solutions tailored to the diverse needs of its clients.

Our history

Maxx Energy was founded in 2010 by engineers Harrison Blake and Elena Navarro, who envisioned revolutionizing the energy industry with sustainable and innovative solutions. Starting as a small consulting firm in Boulder, Colorado, they quickly gained recognition for their expertise in energy efficiency and renewable technologies. By 2014, Maxx Energy expanded its offerings to include power generation and advanced energy management systems, establishing itself as a key player in the industry. Under the leadership of CEO Harrison Blake and CTO Elena Navarro, the company forged strategic partnerships, invested in cutting-edge research, and completed significant projects for industrial clients and government agencies, solidifying its reputation as a leader in delivering sustainable energy solutions.
